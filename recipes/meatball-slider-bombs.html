Meatball Slider Bombs

Ingredients

  • 2 (13.8 ounce) cans refrigerated pizza dough
  • 24 frozen party-size meatballs, thawed slightly
  • 2/3 cup marinara sauce, plus more for serving

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a mini muffin tin with cooking spray.
  2. Step 2: Roll one can of dough out on a lightly floured surface into a rectangle slightly larger than the muffin tin and place the dough on top of the muffin tin. Lightly press dough into each muffin cup. (Alternatively, place a second mini muffin pan on top of dough to easily press dough into each cup.)
